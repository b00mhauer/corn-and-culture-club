The mark — corn ear, fixed (+ a popcorn idea)

The old ear read fishy — that was the downward fins and the diagonal crosshatch (which looks like a pineapple). These use a straight kernel grid and husks that sit right. Each is shown large, then tiny on gold to check it survives as a favicon.
