01 — The Idea

A well-connected friend, in your inbox once a week.

Corn & Culture Club is a social club and a weekly newsletter for people in their late thirties around greater Iowa City. We track fifteen calendars so you don't have to, then tell you the handful actually worth showing up for — date nights, nights out with the crew, and the good stuff to do with the kids in tow.

Curated, not aggregated

The calendars are the floor. The point is a point of view — skip this, rearrange your Saturday for that.

Modes, not ages

Whatever mode you're in this week — date night, out with friends, or the kids in tow — there's a lane for it.

Warm, dry, local

A neighbor with a good memory and a spreadsheet. Never a brand, never breathless, honest about the duds.

  • Keep clear space equal to the emblem's width on all sides.

Don't
  • Recolor the corn to anything but gold, or add gradients / shadows to the mark.
  • Stretch, rotate, or outline the wordmark.
  • Set the wordmark in any face but Zilla Slab.

05 — The Sections

Every edition, the same eight beats.

Each section leads with a hairline rule, a gold line-icon, and an uppercase Work Sans label. No emoji — the icons carry the personality.

The Huddle

2–4 sentence hello. One real observation, zero throat-clearing.

The Forecast

Weather as decisions — patio night, storm backup, pool day.

Date Night & Out

The grown-up lane — shows, dinners, drinks, nights with friends.

Community & Outdoors

Festivals, markets, park concerts, the weekend's anchor event.

With the Kids

Same week, kid mode. Storytimes, museums, slime labs.

Free & Cheap Five

A confident, ranked five. The skimmer's favorite section.

The Deep Dive

One short feature — a preview, a place, a person. Room to breathe.

The Bulletin

Community shorts — openings, shout-outs, "got a tip?"

07 — Voice

Warm, dry, and honest. Never a brand.

Sounds like us

"Free, indoors, and the good kind of tired-out-the-kids."

"Skip if your crew melts down past 7 — it runs late."

"This one's worth rearranging your Saturday for."

Doesn't

"A wonderful family-friendly experience for all ages!"

"Dive into a curated, immersive celebration."

"Fun for the whole family!!!"

Words we like

worth it · skip · bring · free · hidden · the good · our pick · double-checked · call ahead

Words we avoid

vibrant · curated · elevate · immersive · nestled · make memories · "something for everyone" · dive in
