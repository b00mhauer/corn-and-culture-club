## The Deep Dive: How to do Solon Beef Days

It's here. Beef Days runs Friday through Sunday and turns Solon into one big block party, and it flexes to whatever you're after.

With friends, the evenings are the draw. There's live music all weekend, a beer tent, and a 5K for anyone feeling ambitious. With the kids, aim for the daytime, when the parade and the carnival rides are the whole show and the crowds are thinner. It is free to walk in, and most of the fun costs nothing beyond what you eat and ride.

A few logistics. It is going to be hot, so bring water, sunscreen, and a plan for shade. Parking gets tight fast, so if you live close, walk or bike in. Bring cash for the food stands and ride tickets. And go in expecting a late one, because nobody in Solon is keeping a normal bedtime this weekend either.
