## The Huddle

Two things define the week: it is going to be hot, and Solon Beef Days takes over the weekend. In between there are park nights midweek, a couple of good storytimes, and a science morning for the kids in Tiffin. Plan the outdoor stuff for evenings and keep water on you.

## The Forecast

Hot and dry, mid-90s and sunny most of the week. This is heat-advisory territory, so mornings and evenings are your friends and midday is for the splash pad or the A/C. Beef Days and the park nights will be more pleasant after the sun drops. Sunscreen, water bottles, and a plan for shade.

## The Deep Dive: How to do Solon Beef Days

It's here. Beef Days runs Friday through Sunday and turns Solon into one big block party, and it flexes to whatever you're after.

With the kids, aim for the daytime. The parade and the carnival rides are the whole show at that age, and it is calmer and cheaper before the evening crowds. With friends, the evenings are the draw. Tangle Creek plays Friday at 5, the FunkDaddies bring it Saturday, the beer tent is open, and there's a 5K for anyone feeling ambitious. It is free to walk in, and most of the fun costs nothing beyond what you eat and ride.

A few logistics. It is going to be hot, so bring water and sunscreen and plan for shade. Parking gets tight fast, so if you live anywhere close, walk or bike in. Bring cash for the food stands and the ride tickets. And go in with low expectations about a normal bedtime, because nobody in Solon is keeping one this weekend either.
